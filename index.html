Happy to serve you.

We SYNERGY SERVICES , introduces ourselves as one of the complete solution provider for the Garages Equipments,mainly involved in to the business right from compressed air to the Hydraulic systems. We focus on workshop equipment technology & servicing methods, providing durable & cost effective solution to our esteemed customers, we are based at Bhopal & catering our services to the complete M.P & Chattisgarh region. Our major area of services & solution providing are categorized as :-

  • GARAGES EQUIPMETS :-

    Equipments like Air compressor, Hydraulic 2 wheeler ramps, power packs, air piping solution, SS Washing tables, High pressure washing machines Garages Signage etc.

  • PNEUMATIC SOLUTIONS :-

    We deals in the Pneumatic tightening & removal tools like varieties of Impact wrenches,Grinders,Drill machines etc, as well as the required accessories like recoiled hoses, Quick release couplers & adapters, impact sockets, Spring balancers etc, we are having own facility for required repairing of tools.

  • HYDRAULIC SOLUTION :-

    Our main supply in Hydraulics is 2-Wheeler hydraulic ramps with required power pack & solenoid valves, we are also in field of services of Manual/Motorised power packs, Hydraulic cylinders etc.We also deals in supply of required accessories like Hydraulic hoses, connectors, Seals.

  • SPARES & ACCESSORIES :-

    Our sales & service area in this category starts from Hand tools to Pneumatic tools & Hydraulic equipments, we provides services in pneumatic & Hydraulic systems.
